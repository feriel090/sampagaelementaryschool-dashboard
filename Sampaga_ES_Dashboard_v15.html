About This Dashboard
📊Purpose

This dashboard provides a comprehensive 5-year analysis of Sampaga Elementary School's enrollment, attrition, teacher-student ratios, and facility data from SY 2021–2022 to SY 2025–2026 to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯Coverage

Covers five consecutive school years with grade-level breakdowns from Kindergarten through Grade 6, including Special Education (SPED) services. All data is aligned with DepEd reporting standards and thresholds.

🏛️Governance

Sampaga Elementary School operates under the jurisdiction of the Department of Education (DepEd) Schools Division of Pampanga, following the K–12 Basic Education Program with full SPED integration.
